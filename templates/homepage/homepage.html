About Us

What is optifood?

Our product estimates the number of orders your restaurant will receive weekly, so you can plan and produce your delicious meals according to their demand.

  • How do we predict ? It is based on past sales of your restaurant : taking into account factors like weekends, holidays and weather.
  • Why should you care about reducing food waste? It is a major opportunity for your restaurant to save money, enhance public perception and community relations
  • Why are we doing this? Our goal is to achieve one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals : Responsible Consumption and Production

Any surplus food is picked up by a nearby NGO ensuring not a morsel made in your restaurant will go to waste, ensuring responsible consumption and production of food.

1.3 billion tonnes

1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted every year, while almost 2 billion people go hungry or undernourished.

10 billion people by 2050

As the world grows to nearly 10 billion people in 2050, demand for food is expected to grow by more than 50 percent.

If food loss were a country, it would be the third largest greenhouse gas emitter, behind China and the US
